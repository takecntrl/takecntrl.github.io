The Cyber Protocol, LLC – About

The Cyber Protocol, LLC delivers reliable network support, website security, and ethical penetration testing to organizations that require consistent uptime, reduced risk, and proactive protection.

We combine deep technical expertise with clear communication and accountable service delivery to keep critical systems secure and operational.

Core Capabilities

Network Support

24/7 remote monitoring, configuration, hardening, patching, troubleshooting, and performance optimization for routers, switches, firewalls, and infrastructure. Expertise across Cisco, Meraki, SonicWall, Aruba, Dell, and comparable platforms.

Website Security & Management

Secure site creation and rebuilds, comprehensive hardening, vulnerability remediation, ongoing updates, and maintenance—supporting static sites, WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Shopify, and custom code environments.

Ethical Penetration Testing

Scoped, non-destructive offensive assessments with detailed reporting, risk prioritization, proof-of-concept demonstrations, and step-by-step remediation guidance—conducted under strict Rules of Engagement.

Core Values

Integrity & Transparency

Full honesty in every assessment and recommendation, strict adherence to agreed scopes, and clear, jargon-free communication.

Proactive & Reliable

Focus on preventing issues before they arise through ongoing monitoring, timely patching, and consistent care—no reactive surprises.

Results-Oriented Accountability

Deliver measurable improvements in security posture, uptime, and risk reduction, with direct senior access and committed long-term partnership.
