Item #: SCP-SHIT
Object Class: Keter

SPECIAL CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES: Due to the information-technology-focused nature of SCP-SHIT’s containment protofools, iteration upon previously known closure methods is classified Level 4 or higher and subject to review and revision.

DESCRIPTION: SCP-SHIT is an anomalous process that manifests as a potentially infinite time loop within corporate ticketing systems. Once an information technology support request is initiated, it will recur cyclically despite resolution due to managerial insistence, escalating back down the chain of command regardless of actual outcome.

ADDENDUM SCP-SHIT-A: Following exposure to SCP-SHIT, morale degradation becomes evident across affected personnel. Secondary symptoms include spontaneous eye twitching, targets driven to drink black coffee excessively, and the muttering of phrases such as “didn’t we just–”, “I already told them that,” and “for tuck’s sake”. Current containment focus is on mitigating burnout in the loop.

[SCP-████] Echo Signal — Class: Unquantified

CONTAINMENT: None. Cannot contain what does not remain.

DESCRIPTION: SCP-████ is not a file. It is not a system. It is a whisper in the protocol stack. It manifests when an operator questions breach logs or attempts to trace ghost packets.

SYMPTOMS: Exposure causes:

  • Déjà vu in terminal history
  • Auditory echoes of untyped keystrokes
  • A persistent sense that the system is watching *you*
